History of Cricket

Important Milestones

  • 1844: The first International Cricket Match was played between the USA and Canada.
  • 1877: The first official Test Match was played between England and Australia.
  • 1971: The first One Day International (ODI) was played between Australia and England.
  • 1975: The first Cricket World Cup was organized in England.
  • 2007: The first T20 World Cup was held in South Africa.

Evolution of Formats

  • Test Cricket: The oldest format, played over 5 days.
  • ODI (One Day International): 50-over format.
  • T20 Cricket: The fastest format, played over just 20 overs.

Legends of the Game

Over the course of cricket history, many great players have emerged, including:
Sir Don Bradman, Sachin Tendulkar, Brian Lara, Ricky Ponting, Jacques Kallis, Muttiah Muralitharan, MS Dhoni, Rohit Sharma, Virat Kohli.
